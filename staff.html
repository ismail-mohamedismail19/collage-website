University's President

Dr. Alan M. Garber

  • He is the Provost of Harvard University and a professor in the Faculty of Medicine at Harvard University.
  • He holds a PhD in Economics from Harvard University and a medical degree from Stanford University School of Medicine.
  • Before joining Harvard, he was a professor at Stanford University, where he served as the Henry J. Kaiser Jr. Professor and directed the Center for Health Policy and the Center for Primary Care and Outcomes Research.
  • His research focuses on health care policy, economics of health care, and the evaluation of health care technologies.
  • He has been a member of numerous advisory committees and organizations working to improve health policy in the United States.
